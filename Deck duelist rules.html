Deck Duelists Official Rules

A tactical 2-player battle engine using a standard 52-card deck.

1. The Deck Setup

Each player uses a balanced 26-card deck consisting of:

  • 6 Monsters: J, Q, K (2 of each).
  • 4 Traps: 10, A (2 of each).
  • 16 Combat Cards: Numbered 2 through 9.

2. Attachment Limits

⚠️ Tactical Constraint: To prevent "One-Turn Kills," a monster may only receive ONE Attack boost and ONE Shield boost per turn. You must build your power over multiple rounds.

3. Card Roles & HUD

Vanguards (Field)
- Top Left: Rank & Suit
- Top Right: Current Attack
- Bottom Right: Defense Shield
- Bottom Mid: Trap Status
Traps (10 & Ace)
Set these cards on your monsters to prepare for the opponent's turn. Traps must be targeted to a specific Vanguard.

4. Combat Rules

  • First Turn: No attacks are permitted. Use this time to Summon and Shield.
  • One Action: Each monster may only attack once per turn. After attacking, they are exhausted until your next turn.
  • Elemental Cycle: ♠ beats ♣ | ♣ beats ♦ | ♦ beats ♥ | ♥ beats ♠. Successful advantage grants +2 Attack Power.
  • Direct Attacks: If the opponent has no monsters, you may target their Life Points (LP) directly.

5. Win Conditions

Reduce the opponent's Life Points from 30 to 0 or below to win instantly. If the deck is depleted, the player with the highest remaining LP is the victor.
