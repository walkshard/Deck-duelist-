Deck Duelists Official Rules

A tactical 2-player battle engine using a standard 52-card deck.

1. The Deck Setup

Each player uses a balanced 26-card deck consisting of:

  • 6 Vanguards: Jacks, Queens, Kings (2 of each).
  • 4 Traps: Tens, Aces (2 of each).
  • 16 Combat Cards: Numbered 2 through 9.

2. Attachment Limits

⚠️ Tactical Constraint: To prevent "One-Turn Kills," a Vanguard may only receive ONE Attack boost and ONE Shield boost per turn. You must build your power over multiple rounds.

3. Card Roles & HUD

Vanguards (Field)
- Top Left: Rank & Suit
- Top Right: Total Attack Power
- Bottom Right: Defense Shield
- Bottom Mid: Trap Status
Traps (10 & Ace)
Set these on your Vanguards to prepare for the PC's turn. Traps must be targeted to a specific Vanguard on your field.

4. Synergy (Elemental Cycle)

♠ Spades beats ♣ Clubs
♣ Clubs beats ♦ Diamonds
♦ Diamonds beats ♥ Hearts
♥ Hearts beats ♠ Spades

How Synergy Works: When your Vanguard attacks an enemy, the suits are compared. If your Vanguard has the elemental advantage, it receives an automatic +2 Attack Power bonus for that specific battle. Use this to break through high-defense shields.

5. Combat Rules

  • First Turn: No attacks permitted. Use this time to Summon and Shield.
  • Exhaustion: Each Vanguard may only attack once per turn. After acting, they are faded out until your next turn.
  • Direct Attacks: If the opponent has no Vanguards on the field, you may target their Life Points (LP) directly by clicking the PC area.

6. Win Conditions

Reduce the opponent's Life Points from 30 to 0 or below to win instantly. If the deck is depleted, the player with the highest remaining LP is the victor.
